INTRODUCTION

Think fashion, Think FashionTV!

From the very start, FTV has become synonymous with the best in the world of fashion, beauty, trends, and lifestyle.

Get front row access to top fashion weeks from leading designers of the world who showcase their latest collections worn by leading models of the world. Catch all the action on the latest trends from the fashions capitals of the world and all the behind the scene action.

It started as a way for people from the fashion industry to hang out, this led Michel Adam to come up with a channel dedicated 24*7 to fashion and this led to the birth of Fashion. Today, Fashion TV is loved globally.

FashionTV is the biggest fashion medium in the world and the only global 24/7 television station focused exclusively on fashion, beauty, trends, and style.
