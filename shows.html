INTRODUCTION

FashionTV’s worldwide, network presents fashion in
new and imaginative ways every day

From the front row of the biggest fashion shows and runways and the highlights of all the best fashion stories from around the world to the ravishingly hot models, FashionTV brings them to you first!

The FTV+ platform presents 12 stunning thematic channels such as Fashion Week, Swimwear, Models, Designers, Bridal, Hair and Makeup, Fashion men, etc. FashionTV broadcasts a variety of exclusive content on these channels. The following are the details of the 12 channels of FashionTV.

MIDNIGHT SECRETS

Witness the world of sensuality and seduction on Midnight Secrets channel, where beautiful women take you on the journey of sensuality and luxury. With Midnight Secrets, you get to peek inside the sexiest shows, and photoshoots of lingerie, swimwear, beachwear, sexy calendar shoots starring the sultriest and hottest models from the fashion industry.

Watch the Midnight Secrets shows 24x7 available on our TV Network. You can also access the channel ‘on-demand’ with our VOD service any time you want.

F-MEN

Fashion has always been an integral part of men’s lifestyle. Right from the grooming, clothing, fitness, diet, health to making a career in the fashion and entertainment industry, every aspect is interestingly presented by FashionTV on F-Men channel. F-Men is our premium channel made exclusively for men. With F-Men you can get access to the amazing shows related to men's Fashion, Beauty, Fitness, Style, Grooming, Clothing, Men's Accessories, and everything that revolves around the lives of men.

F-Men gives you access to watch the shows 24x7 on our FashionTV+ platform. The best part is that you can also access the channel ‘on-demand’ and on the go with our amazing VOD services.
