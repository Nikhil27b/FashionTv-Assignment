INTRODUCTION

Michel Adam Lisowski is the founder and chairman of Fashion TV. He founded FashionTV to create a global fashion platform focused on fashion, luxury and beauty. He is known as ‘Fashion Maestro’ for his remarkable contribution in the fashion, luxury, and lifestyle industry across the world.

He is the founder of multiple FashionTV destinations that are spread across the world’s leading cities like Tel Aviv, Cairo, Bangkok, Abu Dhabi, Kuwait, Shanghai, Prague, and Vienna.

BACKGROUND

Michel Adam was born on April 16 in Warsaw and is of Jewish descent. He moved to Vienna in 1958 along with his family. Later he was granted a scholarship for Mathematics at Princeton University. Fashion has always been closer to his heart.

Michel Adam later started his own franchise "love-f-cafe" in various European cities such as Monaco and Milan. He returned to Vienna with the opening of the café on the ground floor of the Hotel Bristol next to the opera house in 2013, which became very popular among the fashion community. This café became a popular destination for many fashion shows, afterparties and other fashion events. This is how Michel Adam put the foundation of FashionTV’s massive empire.

JOURNEY

  1. his journey began many years ago when he travelled from poland to vienna and decided to venture into a subject that was the closest to his heart which is fashion.

  2. He started with the idea of Caf Fashion, which became a huge hit with people from the fashion world.

  3. He started his own textile business in Thailand under a company called the Eden Group. Before he started Fashion TV, he opened Caf Fashion, one of his most successful projects.

  4. It started as a way for people from the fashion industry to hang out, this led Lisowski to come up with a channel dedicated 24*7 to fashion and this led to the birth of FashionTV. Today, Fashion TV is loved globally and is the world’s largest fashion media.
